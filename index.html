ALTCOIN CHECK
Before you buy an altcoin…
Give yourself 10 seconds. Save months of pain.
Ask yourself this.
Not “will it pump?” — something more important.
Who is BUYING it?
Real demand or only your timeline?
Who buys after you?
If you can’t name the next buyer…
Where is the LIQUIDITY?
Can you exit without nuking the chart?
Where are you WRONG?
Invalidation + size. Or it’s a lottery ticket.
REALITY
If you can’t answer all four,
you’re not early — you’re exit liquidity.
